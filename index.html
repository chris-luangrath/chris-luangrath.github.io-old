About this website

Welcome Home


I made this website when I was learning with Code to Success, a program designed to help students get a head start in their coding experience. I figured out how to use HTML, CSS, Javascript, GitHub, and many other things while I was with them. However, the site that I made was just filled with pictures of things that I liked and projects that I made to practice my skills. However, for the most part, I mostly used C# and C++ because I was learning those in high school and competing in SkillsUSA. Web design wasn't really a part of my day-to-day, and this site was more or less abandoned. That being said, I'm a college student now, and this website can help me express who I am and what my goals are. Plus, it's important for programmers to have a portfolio of things that they can do. Thus, I need to fix this website up. This will be a project that I need to work on, alongside homework and responsibilities, so stay tuned! Sorry it's a bit of an eyesore at the moment, but it will look much better in due time, trust me!
